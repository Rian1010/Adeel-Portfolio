About Me

It is my goal make this world a better place for animals and provide food for people in need. Afterall, every living being deserves love, food, and shelter. That is why I love my job so much!

Throughout the past decade, I have made it my profession to save people in need and stray animals, especially dogs that get maltreated unjustly. Therefore, my team and I have been meeting Pakistani government officials to find solutions treating animals all over the country better. Hence, we successfully start the "Trap, Neuter, Vaccinate and Return" (TNVR) program. We offer support, when people find animals that urgently need to be treated, so that they can turn to us for the animals to be taken care of justly.

In addition, I am a member of multiple organisations that take care of animals in order to save as many lives as possible. Our team also goes to people need to provide them with food every month, as they require urgent support to survive.

Furthermore, I have started my own business to sell electric items and satelite antennas. My experience in business management has been exciting, working with a determined team to grow together with the business and install antennas in people's homes.

Now, my path continues ambitiously to save lives where possible and to make this world a better place.
